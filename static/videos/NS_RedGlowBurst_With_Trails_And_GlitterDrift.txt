Red glowing light blobs burst from a central point, expanding outward in a sphere. During the fast motion, they leave white ribbon-like trails that last a medium duration. After the trails disappear, each blob fades away, and  new glittering blobs spawn exactly in its place. These new blobs no longer move outward; instead, each drifts slowly from its starting point in a random direction, with the drift amplitude growing over time. They glitter brightly, then fade out.
